**需求摘要:**
Settings > Third-Party Integrations 頁面，當用戶選 "Not on the list" 時，需傳遞和儲存額外表單欄位。

**PRD 定義的 "Not on the list" 表單欄位:**
1. Company Name (text) → **DB 缺少**
2. Provider ID / NPI (prefill from token) → 已有
3. Integration Type (radio + textarea) → 已有 integrationType + OTHER
4. Your Business Model (dropdown) → 已有 businessModel
5. Why you need the data exchange → 已有 businessJustification
6. Service you will provide → 已有 serviceProvided
7. Expected Volume (dropdown) → 已有 expectedVolumeRange
8. Name of your EHR (text) → **DB 缺少**
9. Official Website of your EHR (URL) → **DB 缺少**

**AC:**
- AC1: Vendor column 顯示 Company Name，不是 N/A
- AC2: Integration Detail modal 顯示用戶填寫的欄位

**現有程式碼狀態:**
- DTO: `CreateIntegrationRequestDto` 已有 businessModel, businessJustification, serviceProvided, expectedVolumeRange
- DTO: ehrVendorId 已是 optional，null 時不報錯但 vendor 資訊遺失
- DB: ehr_integrations 已有 business_model, business_justification, service_provided, expected_volume_range
- DB: 缺少 custom_vendor_name, custom_ehr_name, custom_ehr_website
- Service: ehrVendorId=null 時直接建記錄，無 custom vendor 欄位可存

**Comment 討論摘要（2026-01 ~ 2026-02）:**
- Leo: 不支援的 vendor 不應進同一張表，會弄亂 DB；ordering 還在 v1 無法自動整合
